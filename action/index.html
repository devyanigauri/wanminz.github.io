In terms of issue (3) (what seniors can do), we want them to take personal action since that will mean they are more likely to think about, discuss, and vote based on this issue. Come up with three senior-appropriate activities that they can do and provide links to resources. The City of Chicago has a website with suggestions:http://www.chicagoclimateaction.org/pages/what_you_can_do___make_a_difference/6.php On yourwebsite, give them step-by-step instructions for how to get started with links toappropriate city/regional websites. Many of the people at these talks will be elderly and have physical limitations, so anticipate any risks and provide cautionary statements as appropriate to make sure they do these activities safely! Remember, we want them to vote on this issue, so for the third activity, link to a website that tells them who their representatives are and how to register to vote in Illinois.
